# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard
## Project Objective
This project is an interactive and data-driven dashboard that provides insightful, visually appealing, and comprehensive analytics on coffee shop sales. It delivers dynamic and user-friendly visualizations that help users efficiently monitor transactions, revenue, and performance across categories, cities, and time periods.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://github.com/eshita100/Coffee-Shop-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/Coffee%20Shop%20Sales%20Data...xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)

How many total transactions were made?

What is the total quantity of items sold?

What is the total revenue generated?

How much total profit did the coffee shop earn?

How is revenue distributed across the years 2021, 2022, and 2023?

Which year performed the best in terms of revenue?

Which product categories have the highest number of transactions?

Which categories generate the highest revenue?

How do categories compare in terms of sales volume and revenue contribution?

## Project Insights

The analysis shows steady business performance, with strong contributions from major categories like Desserts and Specialty Drinks, and consistently high revenue from cities such as Los Angeles, Seattle, and Chicago. Weekday and monthly trends indicate stable customer demand and predictable peak activity periods.
